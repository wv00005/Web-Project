Welcome

My name is William Vanderboom. I am an amateur artist. This a collection of some the finished works that I have done over time.

King Chesspiece Made of Clay

Description

The white chess king piece was hand sculpted with store bought clay. The "head" and "body" are connected together by a wooden ruler on the inside of the king. The wooden cross on the top acts as a common headpiece seen on chesspiece models.

Boat by the Cliffs of Dover

Description

This was originally a store bought decoration with just the boat and no horizon. I added the clouds, cliffs, and ligthhouse to look like Dover, England
